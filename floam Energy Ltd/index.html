Company Profile

Floam Energy Limited is an indigenous privately-traded oil and gas service company domiciled in warri, Delta State focused on the procurement supply, construction and fabrication of oil fields In Nigeria.The management team of the company has significant experience in developing oil fields In the far regions of Nigeria and is led by an independent board of directors that is focused on safety and cost effectively developing and exploiting the Nigeria oil fields.

Services

Floam energy Limited is well known for its inexcusable procurement services.Thus, we Specialize in Welding and Fabrication of both oil fields and pipelines. Apart from pipeline construction, we ably indulge in maintenance also.

Sales

One of our primary interest is the procurement supply of mechanical, electrical fittings, valves, safety and welding materials etc.
